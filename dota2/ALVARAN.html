Defense of the Ancients 2



Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena video game developed and published by Valve.
The game is a sequel to Defense of the Ancients, which was a community-created mod for Blizzard Entertainment's Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos and its expansion pack, The Frozen Throne.
